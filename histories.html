Fire control computers solve fire control problems. Their solutions depend on:

  • Own ship course and speed.
  • Targets range.
  • Targets baring.
  • Targets course and speed.
  • Wind speed and direction.
  • Initial radius velocity.
Many factors are constantly changing but the computer continuously and instantly solves the problem and send the answer to the guns as orders. Workers operate the director which sends the target range and baring to the computer.
  • Shafts are commonly used to carry values throught out the computer which have two rotations, in one dirrection is possitive, in other direction is negative.
